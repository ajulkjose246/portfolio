About this App

AJCETRIPS is an advanced platform designed to enhance college campus operations through smart automation and modern technology integration. This app includes a dedicated College Vehicle Management module tailored for college staff, enabling drivers to efficiently manage crucial vehicle information such as insurance status, test dates, maintenance schedules, and trip details. With AJCETRIPS, college administrators and drivers can keep college-owned vehicles organized and in optimal condition, ensuring seamless operation for campus needs. From tracking maintenance to logging trips, AJCETRIPS is the essential tool for efficient and streamlined vehicle management on campus.
